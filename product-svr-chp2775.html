CHP2775

  • General Description
  • The CHP2795 is a high integrated power management IC(PMIC) with 3 DCDC buck converters and 2 LDOs.
  • The DCDC converters SWA and SWB supports up to 10MHz switching frequency, which provides the low ripple, fast transient power to load, with small footprint.
  • It features a digital interface as I2C or I3C for programmability, including output voltages, power sequences, protection threshold, etc.
    • Â 
  • A remote sensing feature is included for SWA and SWB to provide high accuracy and to support various PCB routing schemes.
  • Over-temperature, over-current, under-voltage and over-voltage protection features for increased system reliability are included without external sensing circuitry.
55nm BCD, FcCSP ES Silicon Validated
  • Key Features
  • Single VIN from 4.25V to 5.5V
  • Three DC-DC buck converters: SWA, SWB, SWC
    • Multiphase configurability for SWA, SWB
    • Programmable switching frequency up to 10MHz
  • Two LDOs: Vout_1.0V, Vout_1.8V
  • Protections:
    • VOUT: OVP, UVP, OCP
    • VIN: UVP, OVP
    • Thermal: Temperature warning and critical temperature shutdown
  • Independently programmable output voltages and power sequencing for DC-DC converters
  • Output current and power measurement; output current threshold mechanism
  • Power Good indicator
  • General Status Interrupt (GSI)
  • Flexible Open Drain IO (I2C) and Push-Pull (I3C Basic) IO Support
  • Flexible Enable for DC-DC by VR_EN pin or VR Enable command
  • Multi-time programmable non-volatile memory
  • Persistent error log registers
  • Secure and programmable operation modes
  • Error injection capability
  • Programmable registers for customization
  • Idle Power state
  • Package: QFN 3mm*4mm
