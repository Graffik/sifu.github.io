CHP2780

  • General Description
  • CHP2780 is a high integrated power management IC(PMIC) with 5 single-phase DCDC buck converters and 7 LDOs. The LDOs can be driven by looping back the DCDC converter outputs or they can accept a separate external supply.
  • CHP2780 features a digital interface configurable as I2C or SPI for control of the output voltages.
  • A remote differential sensing feature is included for Buck1 to provide high accuracy and to support various PCB routing schemes.
  • Over-temperature, over-current, under-voltage and over-voltage protection features for increased system reliability are included without external sensing circuitry.
  • Package: WLCSP(3.69*2.69mm, 0.35mm pitch), FcCSP(5.4*5.4mm) and eSiFO(5*5mm) with 0.65mm pitch
152nm, 0.35u WLCSP ES Silicon Validated
  • Key Features
  • Flat and high efficiency over wide load range, up to 92% efficiency for all bucks
  • Buck1 and Buck2 can be configured as a dual phase 10A converter
  • Programmable buck output voltage with step size 4.7mV/7.8mV
  • Programable output voltage for LDO with step size 6.25mV/ 7.0mV
  • Fast Dynamic Voltage Control
  • Seamless, automatic, hysteresis-less PFM/PWM transitions
  • Remote sensing at point of load for Buck1
  • Vin under-voltage protection (UVLO)
  • High PSRR PMOS LDO6 (>87dB @ 1KHz, >40dB @ 1MHz)
  • Low-power mode and standby mode
  • Integrate Over-current protection
  • Vout active fast discharge
  • Thermal monitor with high temperature warning and thermal shutdown
  • Control interface: I2C/SPI, GPIO
  • Configurable Power-Up/Power-Down Sequency. (One-Time Programmable)
